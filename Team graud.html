About Team Garud

Team Garud was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate engineering students with a shared vision for revolutionizing aerial technology. Our mission is to develop innovative drone solutions that address real-world challenges across various industries.

We specialize in designing and building custom drones for applications in agriculture, surveillance, delivery, and environmental monitoring. Our team combines expertise in aerodynamics, electronics, software development, and data analysis to create cutting-edge solutions.

With multiple successful projects and industry partnerships, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with drone technology while fostering the next generation of aerospace engineers.
